How to play with CAP X

  • Go to the teambuilder
  • Select "Import from text"
  • Paste the following, adjusted for your Pokemon's set:
    • capX @ Leftovers

    Ability: Illuminate

    EVs: 252 SpA / 4 SpD / 252 Spe

    Timid Nature

    IVs: 0 Atk

    - Sketch
  • The teambuilder will not populate data for the CAP Pokemon, but it will work in battle.

Saving replays

  • This server is now registered! You should be able to press "Upload and share replay" to get a link to your replay. This process may take several seconds.
