How to play with CAP X

  • Go to the teambuilder
  • Select "Import from text"
  • Paste the following, adjusted for your Pokemon's moveset:
    • capX @ Leftovers

    Ability: Illuminate

    EVs: 252 SpA / 4 SpD / 252 Spe

    Timid Nature

    IVs: 0 Atk

    - Protect
  • The teambuilder will not populate data for the CAP Pokemon, but it will work in battle.

Saving replays

  • Select "Download replay"
  • Upload your replay to file sharing site that supports HTML embeds
  • Paste the link to your replay in the CAP forum
